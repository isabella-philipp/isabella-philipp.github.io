The wedding will take place on 30 December 2023 in the outskirts of Quito, Ecuador. We invite you to join us for a traditional yet exquisite celebration according to Catholic matrimonial customs. Celebrate with us a religious ceremony, a reception including food and drinks, and an after party.
Religious ceremony The ceremony will take place in the church Iglesia Católica Santiago Apóstol de Puembo at 12:30 PM . The barroque church was constructed in 1608 during Spanish colonial times, characterized by a golden altar and typical simple wood construction. The ceremony will take around 1 hour and will be followed by photo opportunities in and around the church.
Dresscode. Come in your best outfit and with your best looking partners. Quite formal and elegant, but comfortable.
Reception We would like to welcome you to the reception in the Saloon of the Arrayanes Country Club in Puembo around 3 PM. We will celebrate with local food specialities, drinks of your preference and music of our preference.
Transport. We are looking to organize a bus shuttle available for our European guests to take you from your accommodation to the church and later to the reception and back. Will depend on need and feasibility.
